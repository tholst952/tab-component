Stuff you can do in Minecraft

Explore a plethora of biomes in a vast world!

Use your own two feet to walk and run across plains and over mountains. Craft a boat to sail the waters or tame a horse to ride instead. Find a biome that suits your style and make it your own.

Build and create structures!

Block by block you can collect and build anything you can imagine. Towering pylons reaching to the limits of the sky, underground cave systems with long hallways leading into every direction through water and lava lakes. Build a home or a city!

Find diamonds!

You can find diamonds around Y-level -53, so grab a few pickaxes and work your way to the lower levels or find a giant cave! With diamonds you can craft excellent armor and tools. Check your coordinates often.
